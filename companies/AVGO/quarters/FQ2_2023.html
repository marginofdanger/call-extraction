Quarterly Guidance (FQ3 2023)

  • Revenue: $8.85B (+5% YoY)
  • Adjusted EBITDA: ~65% of projected revenue
  • Non-GAAP Diluted Share Count: 438M
  • Gross Margin: down ~60 bps sequentially (product mix)
  • Semiconductor Revenue: mid-single-digit % growth YoY
  • Networking: ~+20% YoY (AI tailwinds continuing)
  • Server Storage Connectivity: up low single digits YoY (MegaRAID transition complete, enterprise moderating)
  • Broadband: low single-digit % growth YoY (moderating)
  • Wireless: up low single digits sequentially, ~flattish YoY (beginning seasonal ramp of next-gen platform)
  • Industrial: flattish YoY (Asia softness offset by Europe strength)
  • AI Quarterly Revenue: expected to exceed $1B in FQ3
  • Infrastructure Software: up low single digits % YoY (core growth offset by Brocade weakness)

FQ2 2023 Segment Revenue

  • Semiconductor Solutions: $6.8B (+9% YoY), 78% of total revenue - Semiconductor GM: ~71% (-120 bps YoY, product mix) - Semiconductor OpEx: $833M (-5% YoY); R&D $739M (-4% YoY) - Semiconductor Operating Margin: 59%; operating profit grew 10% YoY
  • Infrastructure Software: $1.9B (+3% YoY), 22% of total revenue - Software GM: 92% - Software OpEx: $361M (-3% YoY) - Software Operating Margin: 73%; operating profit grew 8% YoY

FQ2 2023 Semiconductor End Market Breakdown

  • Networking: $2.6B, +20% YoY, 39% of semiconductor revenue — Tomahawk switching for enterprise, Jericho routing for telcos, strong AI infrastructure growth at hyperscalers
  • Broadband: $1.2B, +10% YoY, 18% of semiconductor revenue — 10G PON and DOCSIS 3.1 deployments with WiFi 6/6E
  • Wireless: $1.6B, -9% YoY (-24% QoQ seasonal), 23% of semiconductor revenue
  • Server Storage Connectivity: $1.1B, +20% YoY, 17% of semiconductor revenue — MegaRAID transition largely completed
  • Industrial: Resales $260M, +2% YoY — China softness offset by renewable energy and robotics

FQ2 2023 AI Revenue

  • AI Revenue as % of Semiconductor: ~15% of semiconductor business in current quarter
  • AI Revenue Trajectory: was 10% of semi in FY2022; could exceed 25% in FY2024
  • FY2023 AI Revenue Forecast: $3.8B total (compute offload + networking)
  • Quarterly AI Revenue: entering FY2023 will double by exit; expected to exceed $1B per quarter in FQ3
  • AI Networking Revenue: growing faster than compute offload
  • Compute Offload: very concentrated — essentially one customer
  • At least half of semiconductor growth still from traditional business; other half from generative AI

FQ2 2023 Software Metrics

  • Core Software Revenue: +5% YoY (Brocade SAN decline offset by stable core growth)
  • ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue): $5.3B (+2% YoY)
  • Consolidated Renewal Rates: 114% (trailing 12-month: 117%)
  • Strategic Account Renewal Rates: 120% (trailing 12-month: 128%)
  • Strategic Account Bookings: $564M, of which $133M (23%) cross-selling
  • Recurring Mix: >90% recurring subscription and maintenance
  • Core Software Backlog: ~$17B, averaging ~2.5–3 years

FQ2 2023 Balance Sheet & Capital Allocation

  • Cash & Equivalents: $11.6B
  • Gross Debt: $39.3B ($1.1B short-term); WAC 3.61%, 9.9 years maturity
  • DSO: 32 days
  • Inventory: $1.9B (-1% QoQ, 86 days on hand — consistent with past 8 quarters)
  • Lead Times: 50 weeks
  • Cash Dividends Paid: $1.9B
  • Share Repurchases: $2.8B + $614M for tax on vesting (~5.6M shares eliminated)
  • Non-GAAP Diluted Share Count: 435M
  • Remaining Repurchase Authorization: $9B

Multiyear Wireless Collaboration Agreement

  • Extended multiyear collaboration agreement with North American wireless OEM (filed 8-K)
  • Covers wireless connectivity and 5G components
  • Does NOT include high-performance analog/mixed signal components (sold under separate arrangement)
  • Strategic engagement remains deep and long-term; nothing meaningful changed

Generative AI and Networking

  • Tomahawk 5 and Jericho3 AI: supports 32,000 GPU clusters at 800 Gbps bandwidth
  • Open distributed disaggregated Ethernet architecture for AI clusters
  • Lossless, low-latency Ethernet fabric proven over 10+ years in public cloud and telcos — technology not new, just extended to AI
  • Management does not see cannibalization of traditional enterprise IT spending by AI (budgets coexisting)
  • No long-term semiconductor growth rate update provided yet — AI opportunity could shift it but too early to quantify

VMware Acquisition Update

  • Merger clearance received in: Australia, Brazil, Canada, South Africa, Taiwan
  • Foreign investment control clearance in all necessary jurisdictions
  • Still expect close in Broadcom's fiscal year 2023

Software Segment Dynamics

  • Core software: mid-single-digit % YoY growth — very predictable, multiyear contract model
  • Brocade (fiber channel SAN): cyclical enterprise business — FY2022 was strong up cycle, FY2023 is weaker
  • Combined: currently ~3% YoY growth; could reach high single digits when Brocade cycle turns
  • Long-term: plan for mid-single-digit % core software growth as baseline

FQ2 2023 Actuals (Reported)

  • Revenue: $8.7B (+8% YoY)
  • Gross Margin: 75.6% of revenue (~30 bps above expectations on product mix)
  • Operating Expenses: $1.2B (-4% YoY); R&D $958M (-4% YoY, lower variable spending)
  • Operating Income: $5.4B (+10% YoY)
  • Operating Margin: 62% of revenue (+100 bps YoY)
  • Adjusted EBITDA: $5.7B (65% of revenue; excludes $129M depreciation)
  • Free Cash Flow: $4.4B (50% of revenue)
  • CapEx: $122M
