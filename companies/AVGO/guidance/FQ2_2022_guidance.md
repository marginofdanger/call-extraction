## VMware Acquisition Announcement
- Broadcom to acquire all outstanding VMware shares for ~$61B in cash-and-stock transaction
- Additionally assuming $8B of VMware net debt
- VMware shareholders elect to receive $142.50/share in cash or 0.2520 shares of AVGO per VMware share (subject to 50/50 proration)
- Pro forma combined revenue: >$40B, with 49% software revenue
- Expected to add ~$8.5B of pro forma EBITDA within three years post-closing
- Transaction expected to close within Broadcom's fiscal year 2023
- $32B committed debt financing from consortium of banks
- Broadcom Software Group to rebrand and operate as VMware
- Michael Dell (40.2% of VMware) and Silver Lake (10%) signed support agreements
- 40-day go-shop period expiring July 5, 2022
- Broadcom expects to maintain investment grade rating and 50% FCF dividend payout policy
- Post-close ownership: ~88% current Broadcom shareholders, ~12% current VMware shareholders (fully diluted)

## Capital Return & Shareholder Policy
- Quarterly common stock dividend of $4.10/share maintained
- New $10B share repurchase program authorized through December 31, 2023
- $3B remaining under prior December 2021 authorization
- Commitment to return excess cash to shareholders via dividends and buybacks
- Dividend policy: 50% of prior fiscal year free cash flow returned to shareholders
