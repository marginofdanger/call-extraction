# Broadcom Inc. FQ3_2023 Earnings Call Guidance Extraction
**Call Date:** August 31, 2023 | **Reporting Quarter:** Fiscal Q3 2023 (May - Jul '23)

---

## FQ3 2023 Actuals (Reported)
- **Revenue:** $8.9B (+5% YoY)
- **Gross Margin:** 75.1% of revenue (in line with expectations)
- **Operating Expenses:** $1.1B (-8% YoY); R&D $913M (-8% YoY, lower variable spending)
- **Operating Income:** $5.5B (+6% YoY)
- **Operating Margin:** 62% of revenue (+100 bps YoY)
- **Adjusted EBITDA:** $5.8B (65% of revenue; excludes $122M depreciation)
- **Free Cash Flow:** $4.6B (52% of revenue)
- **CapEx:** $122M

### FQ3 2023 Segment Revenue
- **Semiconductor Solutions:** $6.9B (+5% YoY), 78% of total revenue
  - Semiconductor GM: ~70% (-160 bps YoY, product mix)
  - Semiconductor OpEx: $792M (-7% YoY); R&D $707M (-8% YoY)
  - Semiconductor Operating Margin: 59%
- **Infrastructure Software:** $1.9B (+5% YoY), 22% of total revenue
  - Software GM: 92%
  - Software OpEx: $337M (-10% YoY)
  - Software Operating Margin: 75%; operating profit grew 13% YoY

### FQ3 2023 Semiconductor End Market Breakdown
- **Networking:** $2.8B, +20% YoY, 40% of semiconductor revenue — AI compute offload and networking driving virtually all growth
- **Wireless:** $1.6B, +4% QoQ, flat YoY, 24% of semiconductor revenue — engagement remains deep and multiyear (WiFi, Bluetooth, touch, RF front end, inductive power)
- **Server Storage Connectivity:** $1.1B, flat YoY, 17% of semiconductor revenue
- **Broadband:** $1.1B, +1% YoY, 16% of semiconductor revenue — after 9 consecutive quarters of double-digit growth, now moderating
- **Industrial:** Resales $236M, -3% YoY — weak China demand

### FQ3 2023 AI Revenue
- **AI Revenue:** now exceeding $1B per quarter; represented virtually all semiconductor growth YoY
- **Non-AI Semiconductor Revenue:** stabilized at ~$6B/quarter since start of FY2023 (soft landing confirmed)
- **FY2024 AI Revenue Outlook:** still expected to be >25% of semiconductor revenue (accelerating trajectory)
- **Custom AI Engines:** still one hyperscale customer; engagement multiyear, very strategic, broad and deep
- **Supply Constraints:** exist for AI products — leading-edge silicon, packaging, HBM all have long lead times; orders coming in faster than production lead times allow

### FQ3 2023 Software Metrics
- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue):** $5.3B
- **Consolidated Renewal Rates:** 117% (trailing 12-month: 115%)
- **Strategic Account Renewal Rates:** 127% (trailing 12-month: 125%)
- **Strategic Account Bookings:** $408M, of which $129M (32%) cross-selling
- **Recurring Mix:** >90% recurring subscription and maintenance

### FQ3 2023 Balance Sheet & Capital Allocation
- **Cash & Equivalents:** $12.1B
- **Gross Debt:** $39.3B ($1.1B short-term); WAC 3.61%, 9.7 years maturity
- **DSO:** 30 days

- **Cash Dividends Paid:** $1.9B
- **Share Repurchases:** $1.7B + $460M for tax on vesting (~2.9M shares eliminated)

- **Non-AI Semiconductor Run Rate:** ~$6B/quarter plateau — soft landing as predicted

## Generative AI Networking Technology
- Received substantial orders for next-gen Tomahawk 5 switch and Jericho3 AI routers
- Plan to ship over next 6 months to several hyperscale customers
- Replacing 400G networks with 800G connectivity
- **Tomahawk 6 Development Started:** 200G SerDes, >100 Tbps throughput, 1.6T connectivity (next generation beyond TH5)
- **Ultra Ethernet Consortium:** Broadcom is founding member — driving Ethernet for large language model networks
- Ethernet coexisting with InfiniBand; larger cluster scale favors Ethernet; 70% of switching market is merchant silicon
- End-to-end plug-and-play silicon IP platform for hyperscalers to build AI clusters

## Silicon Photonics (Bailly)
- Proof of concept completed at couple of hyperscalers on Tomahawk 4 (not production volume)
- Production launch planned with Tomahawk 5 (shipping mid-2024)
- **30–40% power reduction** vs conventional pluggable optics
- Silicon yield rates replace mechanical/optical yield rates — more reliable
- Plan: demonstrate at 1–2 hyperscalers, then proliferate; potential major use case in AI data centers
