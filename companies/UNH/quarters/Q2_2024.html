Q2 2024 Membership & Growth

  • Commercial: +2.3 million in H1 2024; employers, consumers responding to distinctive offerings; 2025 selling season momentum encouraging
  • Medicare Advantage: 2025 bid filed with balanced approach; operating comfortably within margin range; prioritizing balanced/durable performance over transitory market share gains
  • Medicaid: Expect membership to stabilize in H2; visibility into majority of rates for 2024; 7/1 rates shaping up well; expect acuity-rate mismatch to even out through remainder of 2024 and early 2025
  • OptumHealth VBC Patients: On track to approach 5 million by year-end; 75% member engagement rate (vs 62% year-ago); post-discharge visits reducing readmissions 10% in mature markets

Q2 2024 Care Patterns & MCR Dynamics

  • Full year core MCR (excluding ~30 bps cyber + South American effects): at upper end of November range (~84.5%)
  • Three additional MCR pressures beyond cyber/SA impacts: 1. MA member mix: shaped by unusual competitive benefit configurations in marketplace 2. Medicaid timing mismatch: between current health status of remaining members and state rate updates; expect to realign in coming months 3. Provider coding intensity upshift: believed largely induced by level of care waivers during cyber disruption; reinforced UM protocols April 15; expect impacts to dampen through remainder of year
  • All three factors roughly equivalent in impact
  • Q3 MCR expected ~84%, "very likely a few tens of basis points higher"
  • No favorable prior period reserve development in Q2 (same as Q1)
  • $800M additional IBNR reserves from Q1 still being prudently analyzed

Change Healthcare / OptumInsight Update

  • Most payment, claims, and care provider connectivity restored; some specialty/custom implementations ongoing
  • Revenue backlog ~$33B (-$200M sequentially; some cyber-related attrition)
  • Change Healthcare is ~15% of OptumInsight projected revenue for 2024
  • Ambition: return to baseline performance in 2025 and grow strongly from there
  • New client interest: cyberattack transformed marketplace; providers seeking innovation and security
  • Rebuilt platform is more modern, secure, and capable
  • Business disruption: revenue pacing back taking longer than initially expected; revenues rebuilding as connectivity restores

V28 & Medicare Advantage Dynamics

  • V28 funding cut: biggest incoming dynamic on MLR at start of year; fundamentally navigating well; year 1 of 3-year cycle
  • 2025 MA bid: balanced approach; assumes care patterns and mix at current levels; fully incorporates second of 3-year phased funding cuts; fully attuned to IRA Part D effects
  • Operating comfortably within target MA margin range
  • Prioritizing balanced and durable performance over transitory market share gains
  • Team UHG responding enterprise-wide (not just MA and OptumHealth) with cost management, technology, consumer agenda

Medicaid Rate-Acuity Mismatch

  • Significant disenrollment from redeterminations left higher-acuity remaining members
  • Timing mismatch between current member health status and state rate updates
  • Visibility into majority of 2024 rates; 7/1 rates shaping up well
  • States committed to accurately reflecting acuity change in current and future adjustments
  • Expect mismatch to even out through remainder of 2024 and early 2025

Provider Coding Intensity

  • Upshift in level of care coding patterns following cyberattack
  • Higher mix of inpatient vs observation after April 15 when UM protocols reactivated
  • Believed largely induced by level of care waivers issued during cyber disruption
  • Not reflected in cyberattack direct response costs (included in core results)
  • Being addressed; reinforced utilization management protocols; expect impacts to dampen
  • Awareness of this activity incorporated into 2025 MA bid

AI & Technology Efficiency

  • Hundreds of AI use case deployments running
  • First wave: existing processes running more quickly, reliably, efficiently
  • Next wave (late 2024-2025): fundamental reimagination of business process; payment integrity as front runner
  • OptumInsight 2025-2027: technology deployment to change decades-old processes
  • OptumRx: record client onboarding at 9% lower cost through digitization
  • OptumHealth: ~40% increase in risk-delegated lives over 2 years with zero headcount increase
  • Expected to generate billions of dollars of efficiency over next several years

South American Operations

  • Brazil sale completed in Q1 2024
  • Remaining South American operations classified as held for sale (natural step following Brazil)
  • Q2 charges: $1.34B total ($220M Chile regulatory action on industry premium increases dating to 2020; remainder noncash FX translation losses)
  • Chile impact: $220M reduction to premium revenue = ~25 bps MCR impact
