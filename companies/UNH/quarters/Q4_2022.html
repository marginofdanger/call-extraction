Full Year 2023 Guidance

  • Adjusted EPS: $24.40–$24.90
  • GAAP EPS: $23.15–$23.65
  • Adjusted Net Earnings: $22.85B–$23.45B
  • Intangible Amortization: ~$1.54B (~$1.65 per share, ~$0.40 tax effect per share)
  • Value-Based Patients Added (organic): 750,000; total fully accountable VBC patients reaching 4 million (up from ~1.8M entering 2022)
  • Medicare Advantage Growth: Up to 900,000 more people; 8th consecutive year above-market growth
  • Commercial Membership Growth: ~1 million additional people
  • Community Pharmacies: 750+ by year-end 2023 (nearly 200 more than beginning of 2020)
  • Medicaid Redeterminations: Expected to resume early Q2; 10-12 month process depending on state
  • Medicaid Revenue Visibility: ~75% of revenue for 2023 established
  • OptumInsight Integration: Significant investments in H1 to accelerate Change Healthcare integration
  • Pricing Trend: Higher than historical norms; reflecting lower 2022 starting point, labor/inflation costs
  • Care Patterns: "Largely back to normal levels"

Long-Term Targets

  • EPS Growth Rate: 13%–16%

Value-Based Care Scale & Economics

  • 4 million people in fully accountable VBC by end of 2023 (more than double end of 2021)
  • In-home services for VBC patients: reduced hospital visits by 15% vs FFS; NPS ~80%
  • Engaging 1 million people daily in pharmacy (lowest cost options, specialty drugs, community pharmacies, home delivery, digital)
  • More recent VBC cohorts reaching better economic position more quickly; "this is an overnight success that took 15 years to build"
  • Half of VBC book is new in the last 3 years

$0 Copay Program Progress

  • Approved in 48 states for fully insured business
  • 1/4 of self-funded employers have added offering for employees; number expected to rise
  • Covers insulin, epinephrine, and other life-saving medications

Surest (Commercial Innovation)

  • 1 in 9 national accounts now offer Surest (up from 1 in 25 two years ago)
  • Provides first-dollar coverage, high transparency into quality and cost
  • Momentum "strong and building"

Change Healthcare / OptumInsight Integration

  • "New OptumInsight" expected to emerge through 2023
  • 2 new comprehensive health system partnerships since November: Northern Light Health (Maine), Owensboro Health (Kentucky)
  • Services include IT, revenue cycle management, analytics, supply chain tools
  • Synergies primarily from complementary growth
  • Accelerating integration investments; most financial benefit from complementary growth

HouseCalls & Consumer Initiatives

  • HouseCalls NPS: 75; driving improving retention levels
  • 2023: expanding vaccinations, testing services, social determinants of health resources
  • Advocacy solutions: 42% increase in closing gaps in care, up to 15% lower ER visits, 10%+ increase in clinical program enrollment
  • U Card being introduced to all individual MA members in 2023

OptumRx / Biosimilar Strategy

  • HUMIRA biosimilar contracting strategy: provides choice without disruption; ensures lower cost for all users (brand or biosimilar)
  • Multiyear strategy as manufacturers compete on clinical criteria, product attributes, economics
  • Specialty pharmacy: 30%+ higher self-service
  • PBM product adoption: up 40% YoY
  • Record selling seasons; customer retention and new wins among highest ever
  • Community pharmacies supporting central fill due to script volume scale

Medicaid Redeterminations

  • PHE ending; redeterminations expected early Q2 2023
  • Process: 10-12 months depending on state
  • Revenue visibility: ~75% for 2023; states took all factors into account when setting rates
  • Objective: ensure continuous access to benefits; when completed, expect to serve more people than today across state-based, commercial, and exchange offerings
  • UHC expanding into 4 new exchange states; footprint going from ~40% to ~2/3 of addressable market
