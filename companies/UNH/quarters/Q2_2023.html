Q2 2023 Membership & Growth

  • People Served (YoY): ~1.6 million more across commercial and public sector programs
  • Medicare Advantage Growth: Over 900,000 expected for full year
  • Commercial: Nearly 500,000 added in H1; 2024 selling season tracking favorably
  • Medicaid: Strong performance; supporting state redetermination processes
  • OptumHealth VBC Patients: More than 4 million; +900,000 over prior year; significant number with complex needs
  • Behavioral Health Network: Expanded by tens of thousands of care professionals this year; ambulatory services in 37 states

Q2 2023 Care Patterns

  • Senior outpatient care activity: a few hundred basis points above expectations
  • Orthopedic and cardiac procedures: "increased far above that level of variation"
  • Appears to be deferred care; higher conversion rate of recommended-to-completed surgeries; post-COVID comfort returning to facilities
  • Care activity levels stabilized and expected to persist
  • Behavioral: double-digit increase YoY in percentage of people accessing care; broad-based across age spectrum (30s, 40s, 50s); anxiety, depression, substance use disorder
  • Medicaid and commercial populations: consistent with expectations
  • No evidence of increasing acuity in cancer, cardiovascular, or other areas

Senior Outpatient Care Activity

  • Elevated outpatient surgery activity among seniors, notably orthopedic and cardiac procedures
  • Appears to reflect deferred procedures; higher conversion rate post-recommendation
  • Drivers: increased surgical supply/capacity and reduced COVID-era reticence to enter facilities
  • Activity levels have stabilized since intra-quarter discussion (several weeks prior)
  • Expected to persist; priced into 2024 MA bids
  • Not driven by benefit changes; acuity, benefits, membership mix all tracking normally
  • Far larger factor in 2024 bid design than outpatient trend was the CMS risk model changes

Behavioral Health Utilization

  • Double-digit increase YoY in people accessing behavioral care
  • Broad-based across age spectrum (not just young people); 30-50 year olds accessing care for anxiety, depression, substance use disorder
  • OptumHealth expanded behavioral network; ambulatory services in 37 states
  • Self-paced digital modules for anxiety/depression with therapist add-on
  • Designing future benefits assuming behavioral demand continues rising
  • Impact shows within OptumHealth margin (behavioral resides within OptumHealth)

OptumHealth Margin Dynamics

  • Q2 margin compression from three sources: (1) senior outpatient trend, (2) behavioral growth, (3) strong complex patient membership growth
  • Complex patient onboarding creates short-term margin pressure but "extraordinarily important foundation stone for the future"
  • Expect margins to strengthen into 2024; believe current trends captured in pricing
  • Longer engagement with patients improves both outcomes and economics
  • Growth prioritized over short-term margin; "I'll take the growth all day long"

2024 Medicare Advantage Bid Design

  • Bids submitted in June; designed with nimble process to accommodate late-developing trends
  • Risk model changes were biggest factor shaping bids (larger than outpatient trend)
  • Assumed elevated outpatient care patterns would remain durable into 2024
  • Preserved benefit stability: $0 co-pays for primary care visits, $0 Tier 1 drugs, $0 monthly premiums where existing, level out-of-pocket maximums
  • Confident in above-market growth for 2024

Affordability & Site of Service

  • ASC procedures cost ~half as much as traditional settings with comparable outcomes; hundreds of dollars in consumer OOP savings per procedure
  • ASC NPS approaching 90%
  • Specialty drug management programs: up to 20% savings for clients implementing full suite
  • Price Edge tool delivering millions in consumer OOP savings
  • $0 copay initiative adopted by 500+ self-funded customers; increasing adherence, saving millions

Biosimilar Strategy

  • AMGEVITA launched at parity with HUMIRA earlier this year
  • Added Cyltezo and Hyrimoz to standard formulary at parity
  • Increased competition expected to deliver double-digit savings for customers

Optum Financial Integrated Card

  • Rolled out broadly at start of 2023
  • 13 million+ consumers issued the card
  • Enables seamless access to benefits, programs, and rewards
  • Strong adoption and satisfaction; simplifies senior benefit navigation

Amedisys Acquisition

  • Agreement reached; early stage of regulatory process
  • Strategic rationale: expand home health capabilities for value-based care
  • Complements LHC Group (recently acquired)
  • No integration specifics shared at this stage

AI & Technology Initiatives

  • Generative AI for medical appeal letter writing efficiency
  • NLP and AI for provider search optimization across digital properties
  • AI for payment integrity models and waste/fraud/abuse detection
  • Benefit bots reducing call center labor for simple questions (coverage queries, deductible status)
  • Optimistic about long-term potential given UNH's significant data sets

Investment Income

  • Growing strongly over past several quarters
  • Drivers: rising interest rate environment + active treasury management deploying cash to interest-bearing accounts
  • Coming off many years of zero interest rate environment
  • Some variability from venture portfolio gains in any given quarter

Medicaid Redeterminations

  • Most states began process only recently
  • Comprehensive outreach efforts underway to help individuals retain coverage
  • Commercial fee-based decline in Q2 from loss of 1 large customer (known ~1 year ago; within expectations)
  • Redetermination outcomes will influence where within commercial growth range results fall
