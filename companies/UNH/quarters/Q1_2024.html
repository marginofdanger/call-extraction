Q1 2024 Change Healthcare Cyberattack Impact

  • Total Q1 Impact: $870M ($0.74/share)
  • Direct Response Costs: $593M (excluded from adjusted EPS; includes restoration, security enhancements, third-party support)
  • Business Disruption: $279M (included in adjusted EPS; includes suspended care management, higher medical costs from claims processing gaps)
  • Full Year 2024 Estimated Impact: $1.15–$1.35/share ($0.85–$0.95 direct response costs + $0.30–$0.40 business disruption)
  • Recovery Status: Pharmacy network, claim/payment at ~80% of pre-event levels; medical claims payer connectivity ~60%
  • Provider Support: >$6B in advanced funding and no-interest loans to affected providers
  • Notification: Individual notification process underway; consumer support services being offered

Q1 2024 Membership & Growth

  • Commercial: +2.1 million people in Q1 (largest quarterly increase in years); national accounts, mid-market, and exchange all contributing
  • Surest: In 30+ states; 1 in 6 national accounts now offer
  • Medicare Advantage: Tracking to 450,000–550,000 full year growth target
  • Medicaid: 7.7 million members; new wins in Virginia, Texas, Michigan
  • OptumHealth VBC Patients: Tracking toward 5 million total; 75% complex patient engagement rate (up from 50% a year ago)
  • Star Ratings: 68% of individual MA, 88% of D-SNP in 4+ star plans

Q1 2024 Care Patterns

  • Care patterns within expectations; consistent with outlook
  • Winter seasonal activity (RSV/respiratory) subsided as expected
  • Senior outpatient activity at the baseline levels embedded in pricing
  • No notable changes in acuity or care pattern trajectory

Change Healthcare Cyberattack Detail

  • Cyberattack discovered February 21, 2024; Change Healthcare immediately disconnected systems
  • Largest healthcare data breach in U.S. history by scale of impact
  • Affected: pharmacy processing, claims submission, payment processing, clinical data exchange
  • Response: temporary workarounds deployed within hours; pharmacy connectivity restored first; claims/payment processing rebuilt with enhanced security
  • Provider financial support: >$6B advanced funding and no-interest loans; "most comprehensive" provider support effort
  • Insurance coverage: potential recoveries not yet reflected in financial impact estimates
  • Security investment: hired leading cybersecurity firms; full system rebuild with enhanced protections
  • Timeline: expect substantially all services restored by end of Q2 2024; some specialty/custom implementations extend beyond
  • Impact on MCR: ~40 bps from suspended proactive care management activities during system outage; additional $800M IBNR reserves as prudence for claims receipt disruption
  • Impact on OptumInsight: ~15% of projected full-year revenue impacted; backlog preservation at ~$33B indicates client retention

Brazil Sale

  • Sale completed during Q1 2024
  • Total charge: $7.086B (majority noncash; FX translation losses accumulated over years)
  • Revenue impact: ~$6B annually (~1.5% of consolidated revenue)
  • Excluded from adjusted EPS
  • Rationale: focus on compelling domestic growth opportunities
  • Global employer business retained (separate from Brazil consumer operations)

2025 Rate Environment / V28

  • 2025 CMS rate notice described as "modestly disappointing"
  • Rate increase does not fully reflect underlying cost trends
  • Company's response: part of multi-year plan to manage V28 transition (year 2 of 3)
  • Levers: benefit design, cost management, network optimization, Star Ratings performance
  • No change to 13%–16% long-term EPS growth commitment
  • Expect to grow MA membership again in 2025

Medicare Advantage Quality & Star Ratings

  • 68% of individual MA members in 4+ star plans
  • 88% of D-SNP members in 4+ star plans
  • Quality ratings support premium position and growth
  • Consumer satisfaction scores improving with digital/app engagement

Digital & AI Progress

  • UHC app: continued #1-#2 ranking in app stores
  • AI-driven prior authorization reduction continuing
  • Digital-first enrollment driving record exchange plan membership
  • AI for claims processing acceleration (critical during Change Healthcare recovery)
  • Telehealth integration across VBC population
