## Care Activity & MCR Dynamics

- Full year 2023 MCR about 10 bps above investor conference indication
- Incremental factors: RSV vaccination driving additional physician visits; seniors obtaining additional care during vaccination appointments; December COVID inpatient admits 50-60% above Oct/Nov; higher cost per case for COVID inpatients
- Core outpatient senior activity (orthopedic/cardiac) stable throughout year; consistent with mid-year 2023 view
- These seasonal/respiratory factors not considered durable for 2024 outlook
- 2024 MCR of 84% +/- 50 bps reflects the higher outpatient senior baseline; starts above 2023 actual
- Leading indicators reviewed daily/weekly; no deviation from core activity patterns

## Medicare Advantage Competitive Environment

- "One of the more aggressive years of pricing" in 2024 selling season
- AEP growth ~100,000; slightly light vs end-of-November expectations
- Shortfall primarily in group business; aggressive competitor benefits drove higher switching/termination rates
- Majority of full year growth (450K-550K) expected February-December (last year: 430K or ~60% of total)
- UHC does well in Feb-Dec selling period: large dual footprint; consumers re-evaluate after AEP headline benefits
- No material pricing catch-up needed in 2025/2026; response to V28 risk model changes designed as 3-year plan
- Provider network: disruptions at or even below historical levels; some deals came down to the wire impacting AEP

## OptumHealth Engagement & Margin Recovery

- 2023 new cohort: started year at ~20% engagement, exited at ~80%
- 2024 new cohort (750,000): starting at 50% engagement (significant improvement)
- Engaging highest-risk complex members at 2x higher rate than same time prior year
- Programs: referral management, Optimal Care evidence-based guidelines, home-based wraparound services
- Payment integrity initiatives across network
- OpEx initiatives: G&A discipline, system consistency, platform unification
- "Night and day in a different position today" vs a year ago
- Confident in 7.7%-8.0% margin target; typical seasonality factors to influence quarterly pacing

## OptumRx Growth & Regulatory

- Best selling year ever; diverse growth across plans, public sector, commercial
- Weight Engage launched: comprehensive solution (medication, provider support, lifestyle modification, digital); live with clients; robust market interest
- PBM regulatory dynamics: respectful of evolving landscape; business model diverse across PBM and pharmacy services
- Continue pressing on: preserving client choice, value-based structures, discount/rebate transparency
- Nothing proposed in regulatory discussions that UNH doesn't already offer in some form
- 130,000 clinicians in Optum platform

## Digital & AI Progress

- UHC mobile app ranked #1-#2 in Apple App Store / Google Play medical category since December
- App installs up 100%+ YoY through first week of year
- Chat volume 2x+ historical average
- OptumRx digital: record new client onboarding with improved satisfaction scores at 8% lower cost
- AI capabilities: real-time admissions/discharge data for high-risk MA member engagement post-ED visit; 300%+ improvement in member engagement rates; NPS of 83
- Prior authorization reduction: eliminated ~20% of prior auth volumes
- Benefit bots for consumer inquiries; AI for provider directory updates; summarizing customer/patient interactions

## Brazil Sale

- Agreement reached to sell Brazil operations
- Expected close: H1 2024
- Charge: ~$7B (majority noncash; largely FX translation losses accumulated over several years)
- Impact excluded from adjusted EPS
- Revenue: ~$6B annually (~1.5% of consolidated revenue)
- Rationale: focus energies on compelling domestic growth opportunities
