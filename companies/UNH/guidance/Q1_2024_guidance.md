## Change Healthcare Cyberattack Detail

- Cyberattack discovered February 21, 2024; Change Healthcare immediately disconnected systems
- Largest healthcare data breach in U.S. history by scale of impact
- Affected: pharmacy processing, claims submission, payment processing, clinical data exchange
- Response: temporary workarounds deployed within hours; pharmacy connectivity restored first; claims/payment processing rebuilt with enhanced security
- Provider financial support: >$6B advanced funding and no-interest loans; "most comprehensive" provider support effort
- Insurance coverage: potential recoveries not yet reflected in financial impact estimates
- Security investment: hired leading cybersecurity firms; full system rebuild with enhanced protections
- Timeline: expect substantially all services restored by end of Q2 2024; some specialty/custom implementations extend beyond
- Impact on MCR: ~40 bps from suspended proactive care management activities during system outage; additional $800M IBNR reserves as prudence for claims receipt disruption
- Impact on OptumInsight: ~15% of projected full-year revenue impacted; backlog preservation at ~$33B indicates client retention

## Brazil Sale

- Sale completed during Q1 2024
- Total charge: $7.086B (majority noncash; FX translation losses accumulated over years)
- Revenue impact: ~$6B annually (~1.5% of consolidated revenue)
- Excluded from adjusted EPS
- Rationale: focus on compelling domestic growth opportunities
- Global employer business retained (separate from Brazil consumer operations)
