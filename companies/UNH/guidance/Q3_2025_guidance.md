---

- **Revenue:** >$113B (+12% YoY)

- **$450M+ in Q3 investments:** ~1/3 UnitedHealth Foundation contributions (not fully run rate but ongoing commitment); ~2/3 employee incentives and AI/technology investments (mostly recurring)

## OptumHealth Progress & 2026

### VBC Restructuring

- **Three Critical Issues Identified:**
  1. Provider network grew too large
  2. Rapid expansion → operating inconsistencies; over-reliance on affiliated physicians less aligned with VBC
  3. Accepting risk in products/services unsuited for clinically oriented VBC model
- **Payer Contracting:** 90%+ complete for 2026; on track to offset ~50% of 2026 V28 headwind through rates + product/benefit adjustments
- **PPO Exits:** 200K lives finalized (majority PPO; exiting 40%+ of PPO footprint); may see additional exits
- **VBC Membership:** Expect ~10% decline in 2026; return to growth in 2027
- **Network Actions:** Fewer providers starting 2026; narrower, more integrated; employed or contractually dedicated physicians where possible; separating from less-aligned providers
- **National Operating Model:** Regionally led, high-performing practices with consolidated locations; reduces fixed cost; drives purchasing economies; aligned technology
- **Remaining V28 Offset:** From engagement (85% of high-risk members engaged in 2025) + operating cost discipline + care program execution

### Performance Metrics

- **2025 OH Margins:** Just under 3% (VBC under 1%)
- **2026:** Margin improvement across ALL of OptumHealth (even with V28 year 3)
- **2027:** Further acceleration toward 6%–8% long-term targets
- **6%–8% achievement:** More back-end weighted but progress expected throughout
- **Star Ratings:** 80% of Optum at Home in 4+ star plans; ~100% of I-SNP in 4+ star
- **NPS:** 90 at highest performing facilities
- **Fee-for-Service:** East region: 3% per visit productivity increase QoQ (scheduling, workflow, patient acquisition); similar efforts in South and West

### Revenue Composition

- **VBC:** ~65% of OptumHealth revenue (2/3 from UHC, 1/3 from diverse payers)
- **Fee-for-Service Care Delivery:** ~15%
- **Services (behavioral, military/veterans, financial):** ~20%

## OptumInsight

- **Leadership:** Sandeep Dadlani now CEO (from CDO/CTO role)
- **Competitive position strong** but not performing at potential
- **Optum Financial Services** realigned within OptumInsight platform
- **New AI Products:**
  - **Optum Real:** First real-time platform for claims and reimbursements in the industry; dramatic early pilot results
  - **Optum Integrity One:** Most advanced AI auto-coding tool; 73% productivity improvement (ambulatory outpatient); 23% improvement (hospital inpatient)
  - **Crimson AI:** Clinical analytics for surgical costs/OR optimization; 6 wins in 90 days; 13:1 average ROI
- **Strategy:** Evolving from traditional services → AI-first services → products → platforms; building AI-first workforce and sales teams
- **2026:** Investments will slow growth but accelerate 2027 results; expected to ignite top line revenue and operating earnings in line with long-term targets

## OptumRx

- **Revenue Growth:** Double-digit in pharmacies
- **Selling Season:** Strong; higher retention and new customer growth
- **2026 Membership:** New growth more than offset by UHC membership attrition
- **Rebate Pass-Through:** ~85% of customers participating in full pass-through; expect 95% by 2027; 100% by 2028
- **Independent Pharmacy:** Increased branded drug payments to 14,000+ independent retail pharmacies
- **Disciplined pricing, transparency and quality outcomes**
