SRE Reporting Change (Beginning Q4 2023)

  • New presentation: "SRE Excluding Notable Items" shown separately from alternative investment return delta
  • Delta = difference between actual alts returns and 11% long-term expectation
  • Q4 2023: SRE ex-notable $748M (141 bps) + alts delta $132M (25 bps) = total SRE $748M (no notable items in Q4)
  • Purpose: understand multi-year earnings power excluding quarterly mark-to-market; industry-wide directional move
  • No impact on historically reported SRE

4 Addressable Markets for Growth

  • 1. Fixed Income Replacement: Rotation of institutional/individual fixed income into private IG; building dedicated sales force 2. High Net Worth / Global Wealth: $65 trillion addressable; "earliest early days"; education-focused approach 3. Active Management Rotation: Pivot from public equity active management into hybrid products; non-binary outcomes 4. Retirement Services: Aging demographics globally; $12T in 401(k); guaranteed lifetime income opportunity - 401(k): ~$1B in 2023; first baby steps of private allocation; limited by litigation risk mentality, not legal restriction - GLI (Guaranteed Lifetime Income): exploring longevity risk hedging; not yet commercial in commission channel; viable in fiduciary channel - International: Europe (40% drop in guaranteed income availability), Australia (superannuation decumulation gap), Hong Kong

Competitive Positioning & Market Commentary

  • Wealth Management: Handful of firms (>2, <10) with right to play; Apollo positioning as "innovator" not "largest"
  • Origination as Lifeblood: Capacity to create excess return per unit of risk is the binding constraint, not capital raising
  • Macro Tailwinds Shifting: 2008-2023 tailwinds (Dodd-Frank, money printing, low rates, indexation) not same as future drivers - Future drivers: public/private convergence, commoditization of public returns, HNW channel, retirement crisis
  • New Performance Fee: Incentivizes portfolio managers net of losses/impairments including IG corporate book; expected net positive for Athene over time
