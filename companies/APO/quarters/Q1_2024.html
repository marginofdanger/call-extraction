Market & Competitive Commentary

  • Fixed Income Replacement: Migration of fixed income bucket to private already happening "faster than I thought"; rating agencies provide signposts for comparability
  • Equity Bucket Migration: Lacks signposts of credit ratings; industry must create new products; "early days"
  • Two Growth Pillars: Retirement (guaranteed income) and portfolio construction rethink (institutions shifting from 20% alternatives allocation to broader private allocation across all buckets)
  • 401(k) / Institutional Allocation: 50% public equity, 30% fixed income, 20% alternatives historically; all three buckets beginning to open to private
  • $40 Trillion Addressable Market: Fixed income replacement / private credit definition now "mainstream" (was pioneered by Apollo 36 months ago)
  • PRT Lawsuits: Expected to chill industry volume; not Athene-specific; Athene strongest capitalized; spreads already unattractive pre-lawsuit
  • MassMutual Partnership: Template for future partnerships; holistic across ATLAS ecosystem; "25% of everything, 100% of nothing"
  • Wealth Channel: Only small group of firms can deliver all 5 components: investment performance, education, distribution, technology, diversified product suite
  • ABC Launch: First-mover in ABF for global wealth; operating company structure enables full origination access (vs traditional fund structures)
  • Credit Quality Philosophy: 100% first lien, lowest leverage (ADS at 0.6x); "time to be levered is when assets are really cheap... time not to be is when markets are tight"
