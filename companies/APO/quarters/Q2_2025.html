Market & Competitive Commentary

  • COVID-Era SRE Runoff: Post-COVID era had extraordinary profitability (very low-cost FABNs, rich assets); amortizing through system; once fully run off, expect meaningful SRE tick up; business itself is incredibly healthy underneath
  • Credit Spread Compression: CLOs and BB crossovers at decade-plus or generational tight spreads; Apollo pivoting origination to maintain spread in areas with less easy access; originating at 130 bps new business spread in tight environment
  • Liability Commoditization: Moving annuities through broker channel becoming commoditized (doesn't require sophistication or high credit rating); Apollo's job to take origination into new markets and create new products
  • Equity Replacement: Institutional demand for AAA as equity replacement happening much earlier than expected; levered share class created for institutions wanting PE-like returns with lower volatility; ICOLI demand also significant
  • Private Credit Trading: Stablecoins, tokenization, trading and liquidity provision expected to expand ecosystem; broader impact in 12-24 months; connected to ACS ecosystem growth
  • Origination Ecosystem Power: Integrated toolbox (DL, inventory finance, fund finance, CLO, HGCS) to sponsors and corporates; consolidated under Chris Edson 24 months ago; crossover impact dramatic; accelerating the flywheel
  • PE Overhang: Industry-wide; not just an IPO story — broader market structure issue and opportunity; other tools and products will be created
  • 401(k) Structure: Primary impediment is litigation risk (plan sponsors forced to lowest cost vs best net return); even without regulatory change, few billion dollars of origination into channel this year; Australian/Israeli/Mexican/Chilean systems as models
  • 6 Sources of Demand: (1) Institutional alternatives, (2) individuals/retail, (3) insurance, (4) institutional fixed income replacement, (5) traditional asset managers (redefining active management), (6) 401(k)/DC — supply of originated assets, not demand, will be constraining factor
  • Innovation Focus: Stablecoins, private asset trading, immediate annuity issuance, streamlined technology, simpler products, guaranteed lifetime income as "holy grail"
  • Team Intensity: 1,200 people in office daily in July/August (vs 1,000 normal); busiest July ever; momentum building rather than declining
