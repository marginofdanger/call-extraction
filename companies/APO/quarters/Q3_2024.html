Market & Competitive Commentary

  • Origination Spreads: Platforms mid-300s to ~400 over (tightened 10-15 bps; from ~400 to 385-390); traditional credit ~375-380 (from ~410; ~60-70 bps compression vs 150+ bps in public credit)
  • Retail Distribution Costs: Migration from upfront placement fees to trailer fees (netted against revenue, not non-comp); reflects product quality/stickiness; no margin degradation expected; contemplated in plan
  • Private Asset Supply/Demand: Expects private assets in shorter supply with rising demand from retirement, individuals, fixed income replacement; fees expected stable long-term
  • Cayman Capital Arbitrage: "Divide the capital by 2... I do not personally believe that is going to end well"; Apollo committed to U.S./Bermuda capital regimes
  • Sidecar Model (ADIP): Clients pay management fees + fronting fee + promote; reflects trust and sustainable high ROE; >$6B raised vs competitors forced to subsidize with own capital
  • Platform Economics: Stand-alone entities; own expenses; strong ROEs; bulk of AAA portfolio; synergy with broader franchise
