Private Credit & Market Positioning

  • Private Credit Ecosystem: "Difference between a hotdog stand and a Michelin Star restaurant" — barriers to entry range from low (levered lending) to extraordinarily high (full ecosystem)
  • Investment-Grade Private Credit: Opportunity for institutional investors to serve fixed income bucket (not just alternatives bucket); 200-300 bps above public IG
  • Banking Partnership: "Never had such a collaborative dialogue with the banking system" — symbiotic (Apollo wants asset, bank wants client)
  • U.S. Financial System: Raises 50% of world's capital; banks + investor marketplace complementary
  • Retirement Services Capital: More Tier 1/Tier 2 capital than vast majority of top 10 U.S. banks
  • Atlas SP: Fully closed; client/employee retention exceeding expectations; >30 securitizations since March; MidCap/Wheels at mid-to-high teens ROEs
  • PacWest Example: Secured financing at "literally AAA risk at almost 10%" — regional bank de-risking opportunity

Athene Business Mix & Inflows

  • Athene's $48B 2022 organic inflows were record; 2023 will exceed $60B
  • 4 diverse channels: retail, PRT, reinsurance, funding agreements
  • Leaving $10-20B of business on the table annually — choosing optimal mix
  • Annuity demand linked to rates but distribution still building rapidly
  • PRT gathering speed as pension plans exceed 100% funded status
  • Japan reinsurance on track; more reinsurance relationships building globally
  • Inorganic blocks market: "final throes" — difficult to build scale inorganically in high-rate environment
  • Athene bought for ~$11B; will earn ~$3.1B+ normalized SRE in FY2023
