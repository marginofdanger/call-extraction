Quarterly Guidance (Q2 2022)

  • Capital Solutions Activity: Transaction fees "run rating at a higher level compared to the first quarter"

Full Year 2022 Guidance

  • Organic Inflows: $80 billion target — "comfortable meeting or exceeding"
  • FRE, SRE, Spread-Related Earnings: Expect to "meet or exceed our 2022 financial targets in the aggregate and each of the pieces"
  • Rate Sensitivity: Each 25 bps parallel shift = $30-40 million additional annual SRE
  • SRE Rate Upside for 2022: ~$0.30/share if forward curve as of April 30 materializes
  • Expense Growth: "Continue growing into a higher run rate expense level" through 2022, consistent with Investor Day trends
  • Operating Leverage: Expect "positive operating leverage as we enter 2023 as the pace of expense growth slows"
  • New Retail Products: 1-2 new products every quarter over next 18-24 months (including ARRIS nontraded REIT, filed with SEC April 2022)

Long-Term Targets (5-Year Plan, from Investor Day)

  • Origination Run Rate: $150 billion (currently ~$100B)
  • Retail AUM: ~$50 billion over 5-year period
  • Capital Solutions Revenue: $500 million at end of 5-year period
  • Excess Capital Generation: $15 billion over 5 years ($5B base dividend, $5B business investment, $5B opportunistic)
  • PII: ~$1/share on average over next 5 years (excluding interest and financing costs)
  • Clean Energy/Climate Capital: $50 billion deployment over next 5 years; $100 billion+ by 2030
  • Fund X Target: $25 billion
  • Alternative Return Normalization: 11% long-term assumption (slightly below Athene's historical 12% average)

Athene Merger & Integration

  • Merger with Athene completed in Q1 2022
  • Renamed primary non-GAAP metric from "distributable earnings" to "adjusted net income" (no impact on historical results)
  • GP investments being transferred from Apollo to Athene, then to a new Apollo-managed fund with third-party capital
  • Purchase accounting resulted in mark-to-market rebasing of assets and liabilities; +2 bps onetime benefit to net investment spread

Origination & Capital Solutions

  • Q1 origination volume ~$22 billion, run rating at $100 billion annually
  • 5-year target to push origination run rate to $150 billion
  • Completed acquisitions of PACE, Foundation, Newfi (permanent origination platforms)
  • Mubadala strategic partnership announced for origination and syndication
  • Capital solutions Q2 pipeline "incredibly strong and very encouraging"
  • Notable transactions: Aldar ($1.4B, largest FDI in Abu Dhabi private sector, IG); SoftBank ($5.1B, IG)

Global Wealth & Retail Strategy

  • Global wealth team scaled from ~25 to 145 employees in past year
  • Griffin acquisition final close adds $6.5B fee-generating AUM
  • Apollo Debt Solutions (private BDC) seeing fundraising traction, especially with RIAs
  • Planning 1-2 new retail product launches per quarter over next 18-24 months
  • ARRIS (Apollo Realty Income Solutions, nontraded REIT) filed registration with SEC
  • Vision: alternatives could comprise 50% of client portfolios (from current 10-15%), using expanded definition beyond traditional PE/hedge

Clean Energy & Sustainability

  • Launched comprehensive sustainable investing platform for global energy transition
  • Targeting $50 billion in clean energy and climate capital over next 5 years
  • Opportunity to deploy $100 billion+ by 2030
  • Marketing a climate finance fund; planning energy transition equity vehicle launch later in 2022

Growth Initiatives

  • Expanding secondaries platform (continuation vehicles, GP/fund financing, credit secondaries, equity secondaries)
  • Asia expansion (Japan, Australia, India) — targeting insurance companies, banks, pension plans
  • Partnerships in life science, software, and hard tech expected to be announced in coming months
  • Third-party insurance business momentum (>$7B raised for high-grade alpha managed accounts)

Credit Quality & Risk Positioning

  • Positioned as "fixed income replacement" — top of capital structure, investment-grade, senior secured
  • Take liquidity and structure risk, not credit risk
  • Run Fed CCAR stress tests and publish results
  • PE portfolio: ~50-50 fixed/floating rate; Fund IX at ~4x leverage (vs peers at ~6.5x)
  • Plans for balance sheet tutorial event in near future
