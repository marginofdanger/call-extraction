## Market & Competitive Commentary

- **Fixed Income Replacement vs Private Credit:** Most use of "private credit" refers to below-IG direct lending (alternatives bucket); Apollo's fixed income replacement is primarily public IG → private IG (fixed income bucket); "happening already and faster than expected"
- **Intel Transaction:** $11B IG private credit; ~50 people across firm touched it over months; template for mega-scale deals (digital infrastructure, deglobalization, energy transition)
- **Private Equity Industry:** "A lot of what has been produced was not the result of great investing, but as a result of $8 trillion of money printing"; Apollo not facing same headwinds due to purchase price discipline
- **Equity Bucket Opportunity:** 8,000 public companies → 4,000; 10 companies = 35% of S&P; 80% of companies >$100M revenue are private; 80% of employment is private; investors will own "equity that is private" (distinct from "private equity" which implies leverage/control)
- **Origination Partnerships:** "Not in place of our franchise, additive to our franchise"; active managers evaluating strategy → some buying alt managers, some partnering; Apollo's role as "parts provider" and JV partner
- **Government Borrowing/Crowding:** Massive government borrowing over next decade → banks pushed toward shorter-term; private match-funded market to absorb long-dated capital needs
