- Merger with Athene completed in Q1 2022
- Renamed primary non-GAAP metric from "distributable earnings" to "adjusted net income" (no impact on historical results)
- GP investments being transferred from Apollo to Athene, then to a new Apollo-managed fund with third-party capital
- Purchase accounting resulted in mark-to-market rebasing of assets and liabilities; +2 bps onetime benefit to net investment spread
