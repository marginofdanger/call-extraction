## Q4 2025 Actuals (Reported)

### Consolidated
- **Total Net Sales:** $213.4B (+12% YoY ex-FX; per CEO: "We're reporting $213.4 billion in revenue, up 12% year-over-year, excluding the impact from foreign exchange rates")
- **FX Impact on Revenue:** ~150 bps favorable (per CFO: "excluding the 150 basis point favorable impact of foreign exchange"; implies ~13.5% reported YoY growth vs. Q4 2024 $187.8B)
- **Operating Income (Reported):** $25.0B (+17.9% YoY vs. $21.2B in Q4 2024; per CEO: "Operating income was $25 billion")
- **Operating Income (Adjusted, ex-special charges):** $27.4B ($25.0B + $2.4B special charges) -- **Beat -- $1.4B above the high end** of $21.0B-$26.0B Q4 guidance range
- **Special Charges (Q4 2025):** $2.4B total reduction to operating income (per CFO):
  1. **$1.1B Italy tax dispute resolution & lawsuit settlement** -- primarily impacts International segment; recorded in fulfillment and other operating expense line items
  2. **$730M estimated severance costs** -- impacts all 3 segments; recorded primarily in fulfillment, sales & marketing, and technology & infrastructure expense line items
  3. **$610M asset impairments** -- primarily related to physical stores; primarily impacts North America segment; recorded in other operating expense line
- **Trailing 12-Month Free Cash Flow:** $11.2B (per CEO: "trailing 12-month free cash flow was $11.2 billion"; down from $14.8B TTM in Q3 2025)
- **Full Year Operating Cash Flow:** $139.5B (+20% YoY; per CFO: "Our full year operating cash flow increased to $139.5 billion in 2025, up 20% year-over-year due primarily to improved operating income and changes in working capital")
- **WW Paid Units Growth:** +12% YoY (per CFO: "Worldwide paid units grew 12% year-over-year, which was our highest quarterly growth rate in 2025")
- **3P Seller Unit Mix:** 61% (per CFO: "worldwide third-party seller unit mix was 61%")

- **Offset:** Efficiencies and cost reductions (per CFO: "we also work very hard to offset that with efficiencies and cost reductions")
- **CFO:** "As we talked about before, that is going to fluctuate over time"

### ROIC & Financial Guardrails (CFO & CEO, Q&A)

- **CEO on Economics:** Companies with custom silicon (like Amazon with Trainium) "are going to have advantaged financials"
