## Long-Term Targets and Strategic Vision

### Software Platform Long-Term Growth Target
- **Long-Term Growth Goal:** 20% to 30% annual revenue growth -- **REITERATED** from Q2 2024 call; CEO: "Last quarter, I shared our confidence in achieving 20% to 30% year-over-year growth for the foreseeable future"
- **Growth Goal Breakdown:**
  1. **Organic self-learning:** 4%-5% per quarter from self-learning and market growth (CEO: "We continue to expect 4% to 5% quarterly growth through self-learning and market growth")
  2. **Step-function AXON enhancements:** Occasional unpredictable breakthroughs that push growth above baseline (CEO: "with occasional step changes resulting from enhancements to our AXON algorithm")
  3. **New verticals (e-commerce/web):** Additive to the 20%-30% base which is gaming-only (CEO: "While we remain confident in 20% to 30% growth for mobile gaming advertisers alone")
- **Upside Scenario:** CEO: "If we're able to continuously improve the technology over the years faster than one material enhancement a year, then the growth rate is going to be higher than 30%"
- **Current Run Rate:** Software Platform at ~$3.3B advertising revenue run rate (CEO reference)

### Apps Segment Long-Term Outlook
- **Apps Expected to Be Stable:** CFO: "We continue to expect the Apps business to be stable in the future"
- **Divestiture Stance:** CFO: "we've communicated in the past about the Apps business that we've optimized that for profitability, and we're comfortable in divesting that business if the opportunity arises"

### Capital Allocation Priorities (3 Key Areas)
1. **Organic Growth:** Recruiting top talent and investing in technology "in the most lean and cost-efficient way possible"
2. **Share Management:** Ongoing repurchases funded from free cash flow and prudent equity grants; $2.3B remaining authorization
3. **Capital Foundation:** Sufficient liquidity to support financial flexibility with net debt leverage ratio below 2x

### Earnings Communication Shift
- **Annual Shareholder Letter:** Transitioning from quarterly to annual shareholder letters; financial results and guidance will still be released quarterly

## AXON / Core Technology Update

- **Step-Function Enhancement in Q3:** CEO confirmed a material step change occurred in Q3 driven by AXON advancements; growth this quarter was "entirely on the gaming side"
- **Early Stages of AI:** CEO: "we are in the early stages of AI software development, both within our company and in the broader industry. We expect ongoing research advancements to continue driving our technology forward"
- **Multiple Enhancements in 2024:** CEO: "we've had multiple of those [step-function model enhancements] this year. So obviously, the growth rate is over 30%"
- **Growth Framework Reiterated:** 20% annual growth = just being in the gaming market; 30% = one material enhancement per year; >30% = multiple enhancements per year
- **No Demand Constraint:** CEO: "There is no constraint on demand. There's no constraint on interest from our advertisers"; "every advertiser has more budget interest in spending on our platform than we can deliver on a daily basis"
- **Automated System:** Growth improvements apply uniformly across all advertisers; CEO: "When we get these step functions, they all have a tolerance to spend more"
- **GPU Infrastructure:** One of the largest GPU deployments in the world; investing ahead of needs -- "we're investing in infrastructure that is quarters ahead of where we are today in terms of consumption"
- **Google Cloud Renewal:** Renewed contract earlier than planned to secure capacity for future growth; Google described as a "great partner" on the cloud side

## E-Commerce / Web Advertising Expansion

- **Status:** Still in pilot as of Q3 2024; CEO: "it's too early for e-commerce to make a financial impact that's material"
- **Product Quality:** CEO: "It's the best product I've ever seen released by us, fastest growing" and "the most exciting product that we've ever launched"
- **Early Results Exceeded Expectations:** CEO: "Early data has exceeded our expectations, with the advertisers in the pilot seeing substantial returns, often surpassing those from other media channels and, in many cases, experiencing nearly 100% incrementality from our traffic"
- **Pilot Scale:** "tens to hundreds total shops" in the pilot currently
- **Timeline for General Availability:** Self-service platform launching "some point next year" (2025); CEO: "we're still on track"
- **Resource Reallocation:** Streamlined resources and reallocating talent from other initiatives to e-commerce go-to-market team
- **2025 Impact:** CEO: "We're increasingly confident this vertical will scale significantly in 2025 and become a strong contributor for us over the next year and beyond"; defined "material" as 10%+ of revenue -- "for e-commerce to really drive material impact, and let's define that by 10% plus, is going to take some time to ramp up to"
- **Go-to-Market Approach:** Organic, not sales-heavy; CEO: "We're not going to invest heavily in sales, and we're going to organically grow over the years"
- **Competitive Position:** Greenfield opportunity; CEO: "I don't think, to date, anyone has delivered a solution inside mobile games for categories outside of gaming"
- **Incrementality:** Nearly 100% incremental for advertisers; new audience that advertisers can't otherwise reach in that moment
- **Beyond E-Commerce Vision:** CEO: "10 years from now, we think every advertiser that has a transactional model...can buy on our platform and do it at scale"; not limited to e-commerce -- going to "the entirety of the rest of the categories of advertising that matter"
- **Additive to Long-Term Targets:** E-commerce is additive to the 20%-30% gaming-only growth target
- **Bottleneck:** Not technology; only human capital and automation capacity -- CEO: "we just don't have the capacity yet in terms of human capital and every solution component automated to scale it out as quickly as the market demands"

## AXON Licensing

- **Status:** Still being worked on but deprioritized relative to e-commerce; CEO: "it's sort of longer-term focus, not short-term focus"
- **Resource Allocation:** All hands on deck for e-commerce scale-out; AXON licensing will come "secondary to the opportunity in front of us"

## Generative AI / Creative Personalization

- **On Road Map:** CEO: "It's absolutely on our road map. It's super exciting"
- **Vision:** Today serving static advertisements to 1.4B daily actives; over time, ad creative could become personalized, which "would have a material lift to our business"
- **Timeline:** Not in the immediate future; CEO: "we can't time it for you, nor can we size it"

---

## Supply Expansion / CTV / Wurl

- **Current Supply:** 1.4B daily active users; supply is not currently a constraint
- **Wurl / CTV:** Acquired Wurl to tap into CTV; "That category today still doesn't have a really strong performance marketing platform"; appealing long-term opportunity
- **5-Year Vision:** CEO: "if we fast forward 5 years from now, we're certainly going to have more supply reach than just the mobile gaming side"

---

## Mobile Gaming Market Dynamics

- **Market Share vs. Market Creation:** CEO: "We're really big in gaming at this point. So it can't be market share gains because there's not a whole lot to go take. We've created a bigger market"
- **Growth Driver:** UA spend unlocking; as platform improves, it unlocks more advertiser dollars -- not shifting from other channels but creating incremental spend
- **Advertiser Behavior:** CEO: "They go, like, 'I can double my spend on AppLovin and keep spending on these other channels to expand my business and grow it faster'"
- **TAM Expansion:** Gaming TAM returning to healthy growth driven by UA spending improvements on AppLovin's platform
- **Audience Scale:** 1.4B daily actives; 45-minute component of the 4-5 hours/day of mobile device time spent

---

## iOS Probabilistic Billing

- **Switch Made in August 2024:** Moved to probabilistic billing on iOS to make it more accurate and aligned with how advertisers track
- **Impact:** "Not really" notable; CEO: "the deltas were nominal"

## M&A Stance

- **Apps Divestiture Open:** CFO: "we're comfortable in divesting that business if the opportunity arises"
