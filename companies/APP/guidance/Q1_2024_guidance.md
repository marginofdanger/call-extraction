## Long-Term Targets and Strategic Vision

### Software Platform Growth Aspirations
- **Long-Term Growth Target:** CEO expressed desire for Software Platform to be a "20%, 30% long-term grower for many years to come" -- first explicit long-term growth rate framing provided by management
- **Current YoY Growth Rate:** ~90%-100% YoY (per CEO commentary), with 73% EBITDA margins -- CEO noted "we don't need it to grow double every single year"
- **Growth Vectors (3 pillars per CEO):**
  1. More advertisers in gaming (not yet at 100% penetration)
  2. Vertical expansion beyond gaming (e-commerce, web advertising)
  3. Ongoing improvements to AXON technology (self-learning + engineering breakthroughs)

### Software Platform Margin / Flow-Through
- **Incremental Revenue Flow-Through:** 84% of incremental YoY Software Platform revenue flowed to EBITDA (per CEO prepared remarks); CFO expects margins to "continue to expand" as AXON technology improves since net revenue essentially drops to the bottom line
- **Margin Expansion Expectation:** CFO stated "we wouldn't expect any decrease from our existing level of margins" -- margins should expand with technology improvements and stay relatively flat when volume is the growth driver
- **Cost Base:** Relatively fixed; no sales process needed to capture gains from model improvements -- CEO: "there's no cost associated with that gain, there's no sales process to go bank that gain, it's just a gain in the business"

### Free Cash Flow Conversion
- **Q1 2024 Actual:** 71% of Adjusted EBITDA (consistent with the ~70% target raised on Q4 2023 call)

## AXON 2.0 / Core Technology Update

- **Status:** AXON 2.0 turned 1 year old during Q1 2024; described as "early stage" with models "still in an early stage" that "will continue to improve themselves"
- **Self-Learning:** Models continuously improve via marketplace data feedback loop -- serving massive volume of impressions daily, data feeds back into models
- **Engineering-Driven Step Functions:** Research science team creating innovations/breakthroughs that produce step-function gains in the business; Q1 outperformance was "predominantly due to enhancements to the models themselves"
- **Unpredictability of Gains:** CEO warned that model improvements "may not be predictable" but "may sometimes lead to quarters like Q1 where we far exceed expectations"
- **Competitive Moat:** CEO stated these systems are "really, really complicated" and represent a "multiyear effort for anyone to replicate"; even open-sourcing the code would not matter because models require marketplace data to drive themselves; compared AXON's data flywheel to ChatGPT's user feedback loop
- **No Limit to Gaming:** CEO: "there is nothing that limits our models to just gaming" -- expansion into web-based marketing and e-commerce expected to improve AI models with added demand diversity
- **Net Revenue Per Install (Q1 2024 YoY):** +5% YoY (per Q&A; vs. Q1 2023)
- **Volume of Installs (Q1 2024 YoY):** +87% YoY (per Q&A; vs. Q1 2023)
- **Interpretation:** The large install volume growth (+87%) vs. modest net revenue per install growth (+5%) reflects the massive increase in advertiser spend driven by AXON since its Q2 2023 launch

## E-Commerce / Web Advertising Expansion

- **Product Launch Timeline:** CEO confirmed web advertising product "will be brought to market this quarter" (i.e., Q2 2024) -- first time a specific timeline was provided
- **Rationale:** Most e-commerce transactions still occur on websites, not mobile apps; many shops lack mobile applications entirely; web advertising opens up a much larger demand pool
- **Expected Impact:** Web-based advertising expected to "build out a lot more demand density" into the platform
- **AI Model Benefit:** CEO expects AXON models to "improve with added demand diversity" from web/e-commerce advertisers
- **Sales Approach:** Product-first, minimal sales investment -- CEO: "we're not going to invest heavily in sales" but noted "in some of these transactional categories outside gaming, you do need some presence, some marketing, some sales, but it's not going to be a heavy investment"

---

## Advertiser Diversification Beyond Gaming

- **Non-Gaming App Growth:** Still growing faster than gaming on AppLovin's platform; trajectory has not changed since Q4 2023 call; smaller base provides more room for growth
- **Gaming Advertiser Penetration:** Not at 100% market penetration; gaming customers worldwide are learning about the platform and adopting it organically; many new relationships are "still in an earlier stage" which will contribute to growth over time
- **Advertiser Behavior / No Budget Cap:** CEO noted most partners do not set budgetary limitations -- if AXON doubles in effectiveness, "the business would double overnight because there isn't a budgetary constraint"
- **Performance-Based Focus:** Company remains exclusively focused on performance-based advertising with no interest in brand advertising -- CEO: "We have no interest in getting into brand advertising. Everything is performance-based"

---

## Connected TV (CTV) / Wurl Strategy

- **Wurl Purpose:** Adds supply (eyeballs) from CTV channel; over 1 billion daily actives in mobile app ecosystem, but ~5 hours/day of TV watching is inaccessible without CTV supply
- **Progress:** Wurl has done "a great job of bringing a lot of supply online"; the opportunity now is "bringing the demand online"
- **Revenue Contribution:** Still too small to break out -- CEO: "It's still too small to break out, so we don't talk about it"
- **E-Commerce / CTV Intersection:** CEO expects that once e-commerce advertising proves efficient on AppLovin's platform, it will "extend very naturally to the e-commerce, to the CTV landscape because the shopping ad could be very beneficial for consumers in that media"

---

## MAX Mediation / Real-Time Bidding

- **Market Transition:** ~80%+ of the market now bids in real-time; the multiyear transition from traditional ad tech to real-time format is "nearly complete"
- **MAX Dominance:** MAX is "by far, the largest mediation solution in mobile gaming" per CEO
- **MAX Marketplace Growth:** Growing "double-digit plus year-over-year" but specific growth rate not disclosed
- **Unity Bidding into MAX:** CEO confirmed Unity is bidding into AppLovin MAX supply; downplayed competitive implications -- described all competitors as "friendly competitors" with a non-zero-sum ecosystem dynamic
- **Data Neutrality:** CEO emphasized there is no data advantage from any company buying into MAX -- "The data that we have available to us is the same data that any bidder on our platform gets available to them"; described as "completely fair, transparent and clean auction"

---

## Mobile Gaming Market Outlook

- **Market Growth:** Industry returning to growth; CEO stated the mobile gaming advertising market (as proxied by MAX marketplace) is growing "double-digit plus year-over-year"
- **AppLovin's Role in Market Expansion:** CEO argued that AppLovin's platform is driving market growth -- billions of incremental dollars spent in mobile gaming on AppLovin's platform didn't come from other channels; advertisers spend more total rather than reallocating from competitors
- **TAM Expansion Mechanism:** Increased UA spend via AXON leads to more installs, cohorts pay back over 3-12 months, revenue stacks, and the whole category expands; CEO: "a lot of the TAM expansion you're starting to see reflected by single-digit growth... a lot of that is attributed directly back to the success our technology is having"

## Sales Strategy / Go-to-Market

- **Product-First Philosophy:** CEO reiterated AppLovin has no interest in building a large sales force; growth is driven by product performance, not sales effort
- **Self-Selling Model:** When advertisers see success, they tell peers, who adopt organically; this has been proven in mobile gaming with AXON 2.0
- **Outside Gaming:** Minimal sales investment planned; CEO: "we do believe that if we're able to go drive the same result where, for instance, in e-commerce to shop, see a lot of benefit from marketing on our platform and they can measure the result... there's going to be a lot of interest"

---

## Competitive Landscape

- **Moat Assessment:** CEO described multi-year head start with cutting-edge AI; even open-sourcing code would not help competitors because models need marketplace data flywheel; compared to ChatGPT's data advantage
- **Non-Zero-Sum Dynamic:** When AppLovin improves advertiser performance, the market grows (more UA spend leads to larger cohorts leads to larger market); competitors are described as "friendly competitors"
- **No Competitive Concern:** CEO: "we don't necessarily pay attention to the competition" -- views technology as unreplicable in a reasonable timeframe; "by the time there's anyone that's actually going to be able to compete against our technology, we'll be years advanced from where we are today"
